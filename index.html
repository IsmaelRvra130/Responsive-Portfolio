About me


Ismael Rivera is a hard working professional. He graduated from Eustis High School, and studied mechanical enginnering, for a short amount of time until having to commit to a full time job instead. Since then he has spent the past 15 years in the hospitality industry, he currently is a lead chef at a fine dining restuarant in a historic downtown location, right on the water. His interests consist of traveling, new experiences, volenteer work, and learning something new everyday. Ismael has been to multiple countrys, been immersed in diffrent cultures, and also volenteers annually for an organization called "Cooking with Vodka!" which is a program that helps the charity group called "Combat Veterans to Carreers"which was founded in The Villages in May 2012 with the commitment to serve our nations combat Vets and their families in the areas of education, employment, housing, and wellness.
